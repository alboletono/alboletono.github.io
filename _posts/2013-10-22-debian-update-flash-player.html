When trying to use the very good site http://www.grooveshark.com I had an issue with my flash player on iceweasel. I couldn't hear any music!

So what to do is:
- update flash player: sudo apt-get install flashplugin-nonfree
- double check your flash player version on line (should be in line with new one): http://www.adobe.com/software/flash/about/
