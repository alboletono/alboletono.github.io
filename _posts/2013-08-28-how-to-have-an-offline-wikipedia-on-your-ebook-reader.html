Create epub

Since 2012 it is possible to create your own book (in epub/pdf or else) by adding wiki articles in it. This could be interesting to have less than one hundred articles in only one book.

Xml export

It is also possible to have subset of wikipedia by adding articles or categories. Then  it will be available in Xml.

Full export

It could be done by mounting the wikipedia on your local pc and then extract HTML content. Time consuming but possible:

  • Get the last Xml dump
  • Inject it into your mediawiki instance
  • Dump it in html

See the whole procedure on http://openzim.org/wiki/Wiki2html#dump_it_all
