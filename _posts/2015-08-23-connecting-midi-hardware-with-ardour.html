I couldn't initially connect my hardware synth using my MidiMate II usb cable. Its ports weren't appearing in the MIDI tab.

In order to do this I had to install a2jmidi:

sudo apt-get install a2jmidid

Now I can connect the midi in from my hardware to ardour midi in.
